Tacos

Description

Tacos are the heart and soul of Mexican cuisine. It even has it's own day of the week in America. Taco Tuesday. It is a staple in most American household's and is very easy to make. It is also very tasty and I can confirm from having eaten it my whole life.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Carne
  • Lime
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Salsa (optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sour Cream
  • Mexican Cheese

Steps

  1. Prep the food by dicing the lettuce and tomato.
  2. Season the carne with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the carne until it is well done.
  4. Heat up the tortillas.
  5. Place the cooked meat into the warm tortillas.
  6. Top the tacos with the lettuce, tomato, sour cream, and cheese.
  7. If you have salsa make sure you add that too.
  8. Serve to your happy fucking family.
